You've encountered a simulated phishing attempt
This exercise is designed to raise awareness about cybersecurity threats. No personal information was collected or stored.
How to identify phishing attempts:
  • Check the URL carefully before entering credentials
  • Look for spelling mistakes and poor grammar
  • Be wary of urgent requests for personal information
  • Verify the sender's email address
  • Hover over links to see the actual destination
What to do if you suspect phishing:
Do not click on links or attachments. Report the email to your IT security team immediately.
